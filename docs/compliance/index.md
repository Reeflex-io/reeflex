# Compliance & open core

Reeflex ships as an open core: the engine, the adapters, and the base policy
packs are Apache 2.0 and free forever. A separate commercial tier packages
that evidence for specific regulatory frameworks — it never adds safety, and
it never enters a public repository.

- [Open core](../open-core.md) — the licensing boundary, component by
  component, and what it does and does not mean for contributors and
  adapter authors.
- Regulatory framework mapping (NIS2, DORA, GDPR) is part of the commercial
  tier and is not documented here; see [Open core](../open-core.md) for the
  boundary and what the open tier already gives an auditor today.
